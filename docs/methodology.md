# Methodology

## Proxima Intelligence Assessment Framework

### Assess → Design → Implement → Optimize

The AI Readiness Assessment follows the **Assess** phase of our delivery methodology:

### Week 1: Discovery

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| 1 | Kickoff call, stakeholder introductions, scope confirmation |
| 2-3 | Questionnaire-guided interviews (infrastructure, data, security) |
| 4 | AI initiatives deep-dive, watsonx readiness workshop |
| 5 | Organizational readiness interviews, documentation review |

### Week 2: Analysis & Delivery

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| 6-7 | Scoring, gap analysis, architecture review |
| 8 | Roadmap development, use case prioritization |
| 9 | Report writing, executive briefing preparation |
| 10 | Executive briefing delivery, Q&A, next steps |

### Principles

- **Evidence-based**: every score is justified with specific findings
- **Actionable**: every gap has a remediation path with effort estimate
- **Prioritized**: recommendations ranked by business impact and urgency
- **Platform-specific**: tailored to IBM Cloud and watsonx services, not generic cloud advice
