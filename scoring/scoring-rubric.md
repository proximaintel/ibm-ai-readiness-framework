# Scoring Rubric

## Scale

| Score | Level | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Initial | No formal processes. Ad hoc, reactive. |
| 2 | Developing | Some processes exist but inconsistent. Limited documentation. |
| 3 | Defined | Processes documented and followed. Baseline established. |
| 4 | Managed | Processes measured and controlled. Continuous improvement. |
| 5 | Optimized | Industry-leading. Automated, self-healing, proactive. |

## Dimension Weights

| Dimension | Weight | Max Score |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Infrastructure Readiness | 20% | 5 |
| Data Maturity | 20% | 5 |
| AI/ML Capability | 20% | 5 |
| Security & Compliance | 20% | 5 |
| Organizational Readiness | 10% | 5 |
| Governance & Operations | 10% | 5 |

## Overall Readiness Score

**Formula:** Σ (Dimension Score × Weight) = Weighted Total (max 5.0)

| Overall Score | Readiness Level | Recommendation |
|---------------|----------------|----------------|
| 1.0 – 1.9 | Not Ready | Foundation work required before AI investment |
| 2.0 – 2.9 | Early Stage | Address critical gaps, pilot with limited scope |
| 3.0 – 3.9 | Ready with Gaps | Proceed with targeted remediation in parallel |
| 4.0 – 4.9 | Production Ready | Scale AI initiatives with confidence |
| 5.0 | Optimized | Focus on advanced use cases and optimization |
