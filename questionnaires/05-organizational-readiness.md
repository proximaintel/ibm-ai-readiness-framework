# Organizational Readiness Assessment

## Purpose
Evaluate the organizational capacity, skills, and culture to adopt and sustain AI on IBM Cloud.

## Questions

### Leadership & Strategy
1. Is there C-level sponsorship for AI initiatives? (Yes/No)
2. Is AI part of the company's strategic plan? (Yes/No)
3. Is there a defined AI strategy with measurable outcomes? (1-5 scale)
4. Is there a dedicated budget for AI beyond existing IT spend? (Yes/No)

### Skills & Talent
5. How many staff have IBM AI/Cloud certifications? (0 / 1-3 / 4-10 / 10+)
6. Are data scientists/ML engineers on staff or contracted? (Staff / Contract / None)
7. Is there a training plan for watsonx and IBM AI skills? (Yes/No)
8. Are business users trained on AI literacy? (1-5 scale)

### Change Management
9. Is there a change management process for AI deployments? (Yes/No)
10. Are stakeholders engaged in AI use case prioritization? (1-5 scale)
11. Is there a process for measuring AI business outcomes? (Yes/No)

### Operations & Support
12. Is there a defined MLOps or AI operations model? (Yes/No/Partial)
13. Are SLAs defined for AI model performance and availability? (Yes/No)
14. Is there an incident response process for AI failures? (Yes/No)
15. Are IBM or partner relationships in place for AI delivery support? (Yes/No)

## Scoring Weight
This section contributes **10%** to the overall AI Readiness Score.
