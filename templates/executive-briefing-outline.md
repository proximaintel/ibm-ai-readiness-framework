# Executive Briefing Outline

## Format
30-minute presentation to leadership team.

## Slide Structure

### Slide 1: Title
- "AI Readiness Assessment — Findings & Recommendations"
- Client name, date, Proxima Intelligence

### Slide 2: Engagement Summary
- Scope: what was assessed
- Timeline: 2 weeks
- Methodology: Assess → Design → Implement → Optimize

### Slide 3: Overall Readiness Score
- Single number (1-5) with visual indicator
- One-sentence summary: "You are [ready/not ready] for enterprise AI because..."

### Slide 4: Scorecard
- Radar chart showing 6 dimensions
- Color-coded: green (4-5), yellow (3), red (1-2)

### Slide 5: Top 3 Strengths
- What's working well
- Where you're ahead of peers

### Slide 6: Top 3 Gaps
- Critical blockers
- Risk if unaddressed

### Slide 7: Prioritized Use Cases
- Top 3-5 AI use cases ranked by value and feasibility
- Quick wins vs. strategic bets

### Slide 8: 90-Day Roadmap
- Visual timeline with 3 phases
- Key milestones and dependencies

### Slide 9: Investment Summary
- Estimated effort and cost for remediation
- ROI projection for top use cases

### Slide 10: Recommended Next Steps
- Immediate actions (this week)
- Short-term (30 days)
- Engagement options with Proxima Intelligence
