## Priority Levels

| Priority | Definition | Timeline |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Critical | Blocks AI deployment. Must resolve first. | Days 1-30 |
| High | Significant risk if unaddressed. | Days 1-60 |
| Medium | Important for scale but not blocking. | Days 31-90 |
| Low | Nice to have. Address in next phase. | 90+ days |

## Remediation Format

For each gap, document:
- **What**: specific action required
- **Who**: team/role responsible
- **Effort**: estimated hours/days
- **Dependencies**: what must happen first
- **IBM Services**: specific services involved
